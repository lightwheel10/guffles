<div id="step-2-engagement-score">
    <SectionTitle title="Step 2: Create Your ICP Engagement Score">
        <p>This is the part that genuinely changed how I prioritize leads.</p>
        <p>Traditional lead scoring puts all the weight on who someone is: job title, company size, industry. That's necessary, but on its own it isn't enough, because a perfect fit who isn't thinking about your problem right now isn't worth much to you this week.</p>
        <p>The ICP Engagement Score fixes that by combining who someone is (demographics) with what they've actually done (engagement). A flawless demographic match with zero activity scores lower than an okay fit who's busy engaging with competitor content. That ordering feels wrong until you watch it play out, and then it's obvious.</p>
        <p>Here's the formula:</p>
        <p><strong>ICP Engagement Score = (Demographic Fit x 0.4) + (Firmographic Fit x 0.3) + (Engagement Strength x 0.3)</strong></p>
        <p>So if someone scores 4/5 on demographics (80 points), 4/5 on firmographics (80 points), and 5/5 on recent engagement (100 points):</p>
        <p>(80 x 0.4) + (80 x 0.3) + (100 x 0.3) = 32 + 24 + 30 = <strong>86 points</strong> (hot lead)</p>
        <p>Score interpretation:</p>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>80-100</strong>: Hot lead - call today</li>
            <li><strong>60-79</strong>: Warm lead - add to your sequence</li>
            <li><strong>40-59</strong>: Lukewarm - nurture before outreach</li>
            <li><strong>Below 40</strong>: Deprioritize</li>
        </ul>
        <p>This pays off in the numbers. According to <a href="https://www.landbase.com/blog/lead-scoring-statistics">MarketingSherpa</a>, organizations with lead scoring see <strong>138%</strong> ROI versus just <strong>78%</strong> for those without. That gap is too big to ignore.</p>
        <p>You can track engagement by hand: check your LinkedIn notifications, keep a spreadsheet, update it as you go. That works fine at small volumes, and there's a fuller walkthrough in our piece on <Link href="/blog/find-warm-leads-social-engagement-signals">finding warm leads through social engagement</Link> signals.</p>
        <p>If you'd rather not do the manual part, tools like Guffles pull engagers from LinkedIn posts automatically and filter them by your ICP criteria. The scoring logic doesn't change either way; you're just spending less of your own time on data entry.</p>
