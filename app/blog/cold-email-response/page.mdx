        <p>Here's the thing: you don't need fancy tools to start. You can test this approach manually first.</p>

        <p><strong>Step 1: Signal Identification</strong><br />
        Find 3-5 LinkedIn posts in your industry that got solid engagement. Look for posts about problems your product solves or competitors discussing your space. These are goldmines of interested prospects.</p>
